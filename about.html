I am
Breda
Malone

Breda Malone is an Irish Interior Designer and also an artist, specialising mainly in Botanical Art. Breda began her career in 1983 in Kilkenny Design Workshops, where she worked on many prestigious interior design projects. She also worked on textile designs for . American company Field crest and was in New York for its launch in Bloomingdales in 1985. Was responsible for implementing shop displays in both the Kilkenny and Dublin shops for Kilkenny Design.

She has a rich background in Design stretching across many areas of design. In 1986 she opened her own shop in Kilkenny where she designed and implemented interior design projects for both commercial and domestic interiors.

About
my Art

Breda a member of the ISBA has also exhibited her Botanical paintings in the Botanical gardens in Dublin, the Waterfront in Belfast and is published in a number of books.

About
my Designs

Her vast experience in design has given her a sharp eye for colour and form, and an innate ability to create beautiful spaces. Her interiors have a freshness about them which transsends time and fashion.
